About Me

Hi there! My name is Faris Kadir. I was born in Trinidad and Tobago, a twin island republic in the Caribbean. I moved to Queens, New York when I was 6 years old. I lived there until I was 16 years old, then my family migrated to South Florida. I spent most of my adult life in South Florida enjoying the beach and night life. My wife and I moved to Orlando 5 months ago because we purchased a new home. Homes in Orlando were much more affordable than South Florida, so it was a no brainer for us.

I currently work as a project manager for a software company. I figured it was time that I took a dive into web development, so here we are. I created this page to challenge myself to use the knowledge that I've gained so far. This page was a test and challenge for me to use Bootstrap. I struggled with the positioning of the elements, but I had great help from the many resources available from UCF and was able to push through it.
